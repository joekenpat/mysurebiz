About Us

Established in year 2000 with pilot projects in Nigeria to ascertain the potency of our innovation, Mysurebiz Integrated Investment Limited is a leading and dynamic all-encompassing business empowerment venture mapping a new course in excellent customer service with world class innovative packages on comprehensive business start-up support and SME management, human capacity development and innovative approach to reduce unemployment and extended poverty in Nigeria and other African countries.

Our team represent a diverse spectrum of professional disciplines, ranging from business strategy, information technology, accounting, manufacturing, engineering and much more helping out as experts to provide one-on- one guide to our participants till they lay the foundation and make the first profit of their business.

Among our numerous undertaking is to manage and foster people- young and old, educated or uneducated on self-discovery and empower them become an entrepreneur. Our organization has assembled full potentials, global standard policies, persistent manpower and ground-breaking structure to achieve our set goals to make any one that gets in contact with us own a business.
