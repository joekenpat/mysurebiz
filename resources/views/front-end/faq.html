ABOUT MYSUREBIZ

Mysurebiz Nigeria is a platform that organises Penny-Wise and Know-How to empower participants start a business immediately after graduation from academics, trade learning or after resignation from jobs. Mysurebiz organises you as a participating student, artisan or employee to keep a token daily from extraneous daily spending or Dependent Cash Inflow (DCI) as Penny Wise Accumulation (PWA) and provide you with an online monthly 100% practical entrepreneurship training called Know How Program (KHP). KHP is structured from the first year through to the end of your academic or trade learning or job resignation period after which you will receive the Total Penny Wise Accumulated (TPWA) with cumulative interest to start your desired business which you have developed with the guidance of an expert during the Know How program. Aside the advantage of business empowerment at its simplicity and information support available to you as participants, Mysurebiz also facilitate a cumulative effect of robust interest on the token you keep daily to increase your disbursable fund by about 50% this enable you access additional funds to start your business venture. Mysurebiz also creates room for partnership between 2 to 5 participants to strengthen their PWA for joint business venture. Should your Business Capital Requirement (BCR) is more than the TPWA disbursement you have opportunity to benefit from a loan up to N50,000,000 as a participant at 0% interest per year.

PENNY-WISE is a smart way of keeping token daily from the money you spend on frivolous things. Your daily keep is 100% guaranteed and Mysurebiz will help you accumulate an attractive interest on your TPWA to start up your desired business within the specified time. PENNY-WISE also accords you the opportunity to benefit capital support up to N50 million with 0% interest. The first key to start your business is to start with what you have. You have Dependent Cash Inflow (DCI) or Work Cash Inflow (WCI) and that is one advantage for your business in the future.
