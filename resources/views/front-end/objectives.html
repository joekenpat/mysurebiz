OUR VISION

To be the No 1 business empowerment venture effecting the highest logical and sustainable cut-back of unemployment and poverty in Nigeria and Africa.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission is to make over 75% participating Graduate, Artisans and Employee become entrepreneurs every year through our all-encompassing business empowerment program and reduce unemployment, poverty in Nigeria and move the Nation up to become a world class capital-producing Nation.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Mysurebiz provides practical entrepreneurship training and unconditional funding for youths, artisans and employees to start a business immediately after a successful academic or trade learning or resignation period. This will enable them utilize their acquired skills gainfully.

  • The scheme encourages and develops entrepreneurship and saving culture in our youths to benefit their future.

  • It imbibes in our youths the attitude of self-responsibility to life than depending and blaming the government, parents and individuals for their failures.

  • It helps and enables the Federal Government to face major concerns of providing infrastructures as Mysurebiz has effectively curbed the issue of crime, social imbalance and economic degrading caused by raging unemployment.

  • Mysurebiz ultimately improves Nigerian industrialization as these beneficiaries will utilize their entrepreneurship knowledge and saved capital to start up a business of manufacturing, servicing, franchising and other investments.

  • This scheme will sustain a Balanced Economy System in all states as the beneficiaries from the rural areas will not necessary migrate to city in search of jobs. Mysurebiz empowers the rural beneficiaries with Entrepreneurship Know-How and the Accumulated Penny-Wise Capital to engage their environmental resources to great wealth and develop their states.

  • The scheme tailors the full potential of our youths to activate rapid development in achieving a greater Nigeria.

  • The vision also creates employment opportunities for other youths that by ignorance or otherwise could not participate in Mysurebiz. This is very predictable because there will be many business that will employ them.
